Sythnwave

80's Synthwave is a synthesizer-based variant of Funk and New York Boogie music popular during the 70s. 70's Disco and other forms of electronic pop were popular at the time but in decline in America, which lead to the mixing of genres, such as Synthpop, Synthrock, Italo-Disco and other 80's-Electro/ Electronic Dance Music based variants.
